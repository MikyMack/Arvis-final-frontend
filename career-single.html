Position Overview

A career as a Junior Architect offers an exciting opportunity to begin your journey in the dynamic field of architecture. As an entry-level professional, you will work under the guidance of senior architects, contributing to the design and development of various projects. This role allows you to gain hands-on experience, refine your technical skills, and build a strong foundation for your architectural career.

Key Responsibilities

As a Junior Architect, your key responsibilities include assisting in the development of architectural designs, plans, and drawings while ensuring compliance with building codes and regulations. You will collaborate with design teams to create innovative concepts, prepare presentations, and support project documentation.

  • Assisting in the creation of architectural designs, plans, and drawings using software like AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp.
  • Collaborating with design teams to develop concepts that align with client needs and project goals.
  • Conducting research on building codes, zoning regulations, and material specifications to ensure compliance.
  • Preparing presentations, 3D models, and renderings to communicate design ideas effectively.
  • Supporting project documentation and coordination with contractors, engineers, and consultants.
  • Participating in site visits and assisting in project inspections to understand construction processes.
