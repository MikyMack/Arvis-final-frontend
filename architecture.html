Residential Architecture * Commercial Spaces * Sustainable Design * Urban Planning.

Our residential designs blend comfort, functionality, and timeless aesthetics — creating homes that reflect individual lifestyles while embracing modern architectural principles. At Arvies, every home is a unique expression of the people who live in it.

At Arvies, our commercial designs focus on functionality, brand identity, and user experience — creating spaces that enhance business performance and leave a lasting impression. From offices to retail and hospitality, we design environments that work as hard as you do.

Arvies delivers efficient, scalable, and purpose-driven industrial design solutions — tailored for factories, warehouses, production units, and logistics hubs. We focus on maximizing functionality, safety, and workflow efficiency without compromising structural integrity.

At Arvies, we design hospitality spaces that offer comfort, elegance, and memorable guest experiences. From boutique hotels to luxury resorts and restaurants, our designs blend aesthetics with functionality to create welcoming, immersive environments.

Arvies approaches urban design with a focus on livability, connectivity, and sustainable growth. We create well-planned public spaces, streetscapes, and communities that enhance quality of life while supporting long-term urban development.
